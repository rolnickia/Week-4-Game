Art Collector !

Game Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.There are four Art Portraits by Andy Warhol below. By clicking on a portrait you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to a computer generated random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the selected random number.
The value of each art portrait is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each art portrait.
